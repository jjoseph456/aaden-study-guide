# 🧮 Math: Diagnostic Quiz

## Part A: 6th-Grade Mastery Review

**Question 1 (Fractions):** What is 3/4 ÷ 1/2?

**Question 2 (Decimals):** Solve 1.25 × 4.

**Question 3 (Ratios):** A recipe uses 2 cups of sugar for 5 cups of flour. What is the ratio of sugar to flour?

**Question 4 (Expressions):** Simplify the expression: 3(x + 4)

**Question 5 (Simplify):** Simplify the fraction 18/24 to its lowest terms.

---

## Part B: 7th-Grade Sneak Peek

**Question 6 (Integers):** Solve: -3 + (-5)

**Question 7 (Equations):** If 2x + 5 = 13, what is the value of x?

**Question 8 (Proportions):** If 3 notebooks cost $6, how much do 7 notebooks cost?

**Question 9 (Unit Rate):** If a local ferry travels 15 miles in 30 minutes, what is its unit rate of speed per hour?

---

## 🔑 Answer Key & Explanations

| # | Answer | Explanation |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1 | 3/2 or 1½ | Apply the "Keep, Change, Flip" rule for dividing fractions |
| 2 | 5 | Multiply 125 by 4 to get 500, then move the decimal point two places to the left |
| 3 | 2:5 or 2/5 | Ratios compare quantities in the order they are asked |
| 4 | 3x + 12 | Apply the distributive property by multiplying 3 by both x and 4 |
| 5 | 3/4 | Divide the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor, 6 |
| 6 | -8 | Adding a negative number means moving further to the left on a number line |
| 7 | x = 4 | Isolate the variable: subtract 5 from both sides to get 2x = 8, then divide by 2 |
| 8 | $14 | Find the unit rate first: $6 ÷ 3 = $2 per notebook. Then $2 × 7 |
| 9 | 30 mph | Double the 30-minute distance to calculate total distance over 60 minutes |
