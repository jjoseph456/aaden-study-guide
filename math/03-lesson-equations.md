# 🧮 Math Lesson: Solving Equations

## What Is an Equation?

An equation is like a **balance scale** — whatever you do to one side, you MUST do to the other side to keep it balanced.

```
    2x + 5 = 13
    ⚖️ Left side = Right side
```

Your job: **Find what number x equals** by getting x alone on one side.

---

## The Golden Rule: Inverse Operations

To isolate x, use the OPPOSITE operation:

| If you see... | Do the opposite... |
|---------------|-------------------|
| + (addition) | − (subtraction) |
| − (subtraction) | + (addition) |
| × (multiplication) | ÷ (division) |
| ÷ (division) | × (multiplication) |

---

## Step-by-Step Method

### Example 1: `x + 7 = 12`

**Goal:** Get x alone.

```
x + 7 = 12
x + 7 - 7 = 12 - 7    ← Subtract 7 from BOTH sides
x = 5                   ← Done!
```

**Check:** 5 + 7 = 12 ✅

---

### Example 2: `3x = 18`

**Goal:** Get x alone. Right now it's being multiplied by 3.

```
3x = 18
3x ÷ 3 = 18 ÷ 3      ← Divide BOTH sides by 3
x = 6                   ← Done!
```

**Check:** 3 × 6 = 18 ✅

---

### Example 3 (Two Steps): `2x + 5 = 13`

When there are two operations, **undo them in reverse order** (peel off layers like an onion):

```
2x + 5 = 13
2x + 5 - 5 = 13 - 5   ← Step 1: Subtract 5 from both sides
2x = 8
2x ÷ 2 = 8 ÷ 2        ← Step 2: Divide both sides by 2
x = 4                   ← Done!
```

**Check:** 2(4) + 5 = 8 + 5 = 13 ✅

---

### Example 4: `x/4 - 3 = 2`

```
x/4 - 3 = 2
x/4 - 3 + 3 = 2 + 3   ← Step 1: Add 3 to both sides
x/4 = 5
x/4 × 4 = 5 × 4       ← Step 2: Multiply both sides by 4
x = 20                  ← Done!
```

**Check:** 20/4 - 3 = 5 - 3 = 2 ✅

---

## Memory Trick: "Unwrap the Present" 🎁

Think of x as a present wrapped in layers:
1. First layer (outermost): addition/subtraction → remove it first
2. Second layer (closest to x): multiplication/division → remove it second

You always unwrap from the OUTSIDE in.

## Real-Life Examples (Island Style 🏝️)

1. **Shopping:** A t-shirt costs $x. You buy 3 of them and pay $5 for shipping. Total: $32. What does one shirt cost?
   - Equation: `3x + 5 = 32`
   - Solve: 3x = 27 → x = **$9**

2. **Ferry Ride:** You split a ferry ticket equally among 4 friends and each person also pays a $2 fuel surcharge. Each person pays $7 total. What was the original ticket price?
   - Equation: `x/4 + 2 = 7`
   - Solve: x/4 = 5 → x = **$20**

## Practice Problems

Solve for x:

1. `x + 9 = 15`
2. `x - 4 = 11`
3. `5x = 35`
4. `x/3 = 6`
5. `2x + 3 = 17`
6. `4x - 8 = 20`
7. `x/5 + 4 = 9`
8. You buy x mangos at $2 each and a bag for $3. You spend $15 total. How many mangos?
